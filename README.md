# Wiring:
|| ESP8266 | ESP32 | OLED |
|:-:| :----: | :----: | :-----: |
||3.3V | 3.3V | VCC |
||GND | GND | GND |
|`SCL`|D1 (GPIO5) | GPIO22| SCL |
|`SDA`|D2 (GPIO4) | GPIO21 | SDA |
